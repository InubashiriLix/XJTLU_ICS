** Core Charactertistics
1. _User should be involoved_ through the development of the project
2. Specific _usability and user expereience goals_ need to be identified, clearly documented and agreed to at the begining of project

*** Importance of involoving users
1. Understanding user's goal
2. Expectataion management
   - Realistic expectations
   - No surprises, no disappointment
   - Timely training
   - Communication but no hype
3. Ownership
   - users active stakeholders
   - More likely to forgive or accept problems
   - Can make a big difference in a acceptance of a product

2. *_Face-to-Face_* group or individual activities
3. Online Contribution from users
