* Public key crypto: 
- public key: known to everyone, used for encryption
- private key: known only to owner, used for decryption
  
** RSA Algorithm:
*** *RSA Encryption*:
public key: (7, 33) (E, N)
plaintext: 1, 2, 3
step1: power src to E
-->: 1, 2^7, 3^7
step2: mod N
-->: 1%33, 128%33, 2187%33
-->: 1, 29, 9 
1, 29, 9 is the ciphertext

*** *RSA Decryption*:
private key: (3, 33) (D, N)
ciphertext: 1, 29, 9
step1: power ciphertext to D
-->: 1^3, 29^3, 9^3
step2: mod N:
---> 1 % 33, 24389 % 33, 729 % 33
---> 1, 2, 3
1, 2, 3 are the decrypted plaintext

*** Generate RSA keys:
- step1: select two big *Prime Numbers*: p, q
- step2: multiply to get N = p * q
- step3: apply Euler's totient function:
  T = (p - 1) * (q - 1)
- step4: select the public Exponent E:
  - Prime Number
  - 1 < E < T
  - gcd(E, T) == 1
- step5: select the private Decryption Exponent D:
  (D * E) % T == 1
  
*** DrawBakcs of RSA:
- the public key encryption is slow
  while the symmetric key encryption is fast
  in the project, we usually use RSA to establish a secure channnel
  then the data transport will use symmetric key encryption
