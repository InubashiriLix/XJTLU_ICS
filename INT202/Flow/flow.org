* Flow Network
a flow network (or just network) N consisits of
- A weighted digraph G with nonnegative integer ege weights, where the weight of an edge e is called the *capacity c(e) of e*
- Two distinguished vertices, s and t of G, called the *source* and *sink*, respectively, such that s has no incoming edges and t has no outgoing edges
** Flow
- Capapcity Rule: for each edge e, 0 <= f(e) <= c(e)
- Conservation Rule: for each vertex v other than s and t, 
  sum of f(e) for all incoming edges = sum of f(e) for all outgoing edges
