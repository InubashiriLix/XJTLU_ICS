2. for each augmenting path п, add Δf(п) to the flow f

   can increase flow on forward edges
   can decrease flow on backward edges)

For each step of the algorithm, write in a table the augmenting path by listing its vertices,

** (2)
What is the value of the maximum flow?
*** Solution:
The value of the maximum flow is the total flow leaving the source s:
f(s,A) + f(s,C) = 6 + 3 = 9
Therefore:
Maximum flow = 9
** (3)

** (4)
What is the capacity of the minimum cut?

| Edge | Capacity |
