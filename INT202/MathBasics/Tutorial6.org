* 4. Let n > 0 be an integer. Prove that n is divisible by 9 if and only if the sum of its digits is divisible by 9.
let a = a0 + a1*10 + a2*10^2 .... ak*10^k
if n is divisible by 9, then
(a0 + a1*10 + a2*10^2 .... ak*10^k) mod 9 = 0
because 10 === 10^k === 1 (mod 9), therefore
(a0 + a1*10 + a2*10^2 .... ak*10^k) mod 9 = (a0 + a1 + a2 + ... + ak) mod 9 = 0
therefore the sum of its digits is divisible by 9
